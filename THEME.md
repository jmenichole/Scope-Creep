# Boundari.ai Design Theme

**Tagline:** "Nice freelancer. Mean contracts."

**Vibe:** Think Headspace meets Contract Law

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Warm Taupe** (`#C9B8A8`) - Primary background, main containers
- **Soft Mint** (`#B8DED1`) - Success states, positive confirmations
- **Ivory** (`#F8F6F1`) - Content background, card backgrounds
- **Moss Green** (`#7A9A7E`) - Primary actions, CTA buttons

### Accent Colors
- **Muted Teal** (`#88B5B5`) - Links, secondary actions
- **Dusty Lavender** (`#B5A7C4`) - Tertiary elements, subtle highlights

## Severity Levels

Instead of aggressive terminology, we use calming language:

| Old Term | New Term | Description |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| Critical | needs-attention | Requires immediate but calm review |
| High | notable | Worth noting and addressing |
| Medium | gentle-reminder | Polite nudge to review |
| Low | awareness | Simple FYI, no action needed |

## Typography Guidelines

- **Headings**: Friendly sans-serif (e.g., Inter, SF Pro Display)
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter, SF Pro Text)
- **Tone**: Professional but warm, firm but kind

## UI Principles

1. **Soothing Interface**: No harsh red alerts or aggressive warnings
2. **Hidden Strength**: Calm appearance with legal backbone
3. **Emotional Intelligence**: Understanding but boundaried
4. **Professional Kindness**: Nice freelancer energy with contract law precision
